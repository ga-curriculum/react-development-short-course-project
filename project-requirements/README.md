## Overview

In this project, you’ll create a simple **"To-Do List"** style application using React. You will work individually on this project.

## Technical requirements

### MVP

> ✅ More than 1 item marked incomplete in this section will require you to re-submit your project. Your app _must_ fulfill the below requirements.

- The app has a minimum of **3 components** (e.g., `App`, `ItemList`, `Item`).
- The app uses **state management** to handle a list of items and includes the ability to **add new items**.
- Users can interact with the items, such as clicking to view details or marking them as complete.
- The app dynamically updates the list when new items are added or removed.
- A **mock data array** is used as the initial list of items.

## Evaluation

Upon completion of your presentation, your instructional team will evaluate your project based on the above guidelines.

- If your instructors determine that the project does not meet the minimum requirements, you may be provided with the opportunity to complete or re-submit it. Contact your instructional team for more details.
